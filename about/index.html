About Us

CitrusWave is a Zimbabwe-based juice brand focused on creating fresh, flavorful drinks that feel simple, natural, and refreshing. We believe a good juice should do more than taste sweet — it should feel vibrant, clean, and made with care.



Our goal is to offer juices that fit everyday life, whether you want a quick refreshment, a healthier drink option, or something bright and enjoyable to share. From citrus blends to fruit favorites, every CitrusWave product is inspired by freshness, quality, and consistency.



As a brand rooted in Zimbabwe, we want CitrusWave to represent energy, creativity, and local pride. We are building more than just a juice line — we are building a modern brand that people can trust, enjoy, and proudly connect with.
